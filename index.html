Navigation Bar

Hello everyone,
For my capstone project, I elected to pursue a tutorial on YouTube. The focus of my endeavor was the development of a responsive navigation bar.Here's how it functions: At desktop size, the webpage showcases the logo, navigation links, and social media buttons horizontally across the header. As the webpage is scaled down, a responsive navigation design comes into play. At the smallest media query, the social icons are hidden entirely, and the navigation links are condensed into a hamburger menu. This approach ensures a seamless transition from mobile to desktop, optimizing user experience across all devices.
